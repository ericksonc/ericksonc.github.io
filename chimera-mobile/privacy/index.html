This Privacy Policy applies to the Chimera Mobile app for mobile devices, together with any related services operated by Christopher Ryan Erickson (collectively, the “Application”). Christopher Ryan Erickson is referred to as the “Service Provider,” “we,” “us,” or “our.” It explains what information the Application collects, how that information is used and shared, and the choices you have.

How the Application works

Chimera Mobile is a voice-based assistant. When you speak to it, your speech is converted to text, that text is processed by an AI language model to generate a response, and the response is spoken back to you. Understanding this flow is the clearest way to understand what data the Application handles, so the data practices below are described in those terms.

Information We Collect and How We Use It

Audio. When you speak to the Application, it captures audio from your device’s microphone. A copy of your audio recordings is stored locally on your device, under your control, similar to a voice-memo app. This local audio is not transmitted to or stored on the Service Provider’s servers. To convert your speech to text, audio is streamed directly from your device to a third-party speech-to-text provider (see How your voice data is processed and shared below). Your device connects to that provider using a short-lived credential issued by our servers, so your audio travels from your device to the provider without being routed through or stored on our servers.

Voice transcripts and conversation history. The text transcribed from your speech, together with the Application’s responses, is sent to our servers and stored as your conversation history — in the same way a chat application keeps a record of past conversations. We use this to provide the assistant’s functionality, including continuity across turns and sessions, and to operate and improve the Application.

Account information. If you create an account, we collect information such as your email address and a user identifier, which we use to provide and secure the Application.

Device and technical information. When your device communicates with our servers, we may automatically collect technical information such as your IP address, device model, operating system version, app version, and diagnostic or crash data, which we use to operate, secure, and troubleshoot the Application.

We may also use the contact information you provide to send important service-related and required notices, and, where permitted by law and subject to your consent where required, other communications.

How your voice data is processed and shared

To deliver its core features, the Application sends data to third-party service providers that perform specific functions on our behalf. Which providers are involved depends on the mode and configuration you use, and each provider receives only the data needed for its function:

  • Speech-to-text — Deepgram. Your spoken audio is streamed from your device to Deepgram, which transcribes it into text. Privacy policy: https://deepgram.com/privacy
  • Response generation, standard mode — OpenRouter and the configured model provider. The text of your requests and conversation is sent to OpenRouter, which routes it to the large language model provider configured for your agent in order to generate a response. The specific downstream model provider depends on your configuration, and that provider processes the text under its own privacy policy. OpenRouter privacy policy: https://openrouter.ai/privacy
  • Response generation and speech, realtime mode — OpenAI. When you use realtime mode, the text of your requests is sent to OpenAI’s Realtime API, which generates the response and the spoken audio. Only text is sent in this mode; your audio is not sent to OpenAI. OpenAI privacy policy: https://openai.com/policies/privacy-policy/
  • Text-to-speech, standard mode — ElevenLabs. To speak responses aloud in standard mode, only the text of the response to be spoken (not the rest of your conversation) is sent to ElevenLabs, which synthesizes it into audio. ElevenLabs privacy policy: https://elevenlabs.io/privacy-policy

These providers process your data to perform the functions described above and handle it in accordance with their own privacy policies, linked above. We do not sell your personal information, and we do not share it with third parties for their own advertising purposes.

  • to trusted service providers who work on our behalf, do not have an independent right to use the information we disclose to them, and have agreed to adhere to the rules set forth in this Privacy Policy.
